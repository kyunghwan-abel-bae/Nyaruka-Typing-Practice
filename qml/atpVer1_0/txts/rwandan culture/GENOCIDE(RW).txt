Itsembabwoko ntiribaho narimwe ritateguwe.
Ni igikorwa kigambiriwe n'abicanyi benshi, kigamije kurimbura burundu
imibereho y'ubwoko bwicwa. Abarikora ntibita ku myaka, igitsina, umurimo,
idini cyangwa imibereho by'abicwa.
Igikorwa cyose kigambiriye itsembabwoko siko kigera ku mugambi nyawo. 
Ibikorwa by'ihohoterwa ry'ikiremwamuntu bigira amazina atandukanye, 
nka POLITICIDE (ukwicwa kw'agatsiko k'abanyapolitiki) na ETHINOCIDE
(ukwicwa k'ubwoko runaka). Ibi ntibivuga ko iri tsembabwoko cyangwa
ririya riruta irindi, cyangwa ryashegeshe kurusha irindi, gusa bitandukanira
ku murava wakoreshejwe cyangwa ingaruka zarivuyemo. Inyito yose yakoreshwa,
inzirakarengane zirimburwa ziba akenshi zifite ukuri gusesuye, kand zishegeshwa
bikomeye n'imigambi igambiriye gutsembatsemba ubuzima bwazo.
Imurika ry'urwibutso rwa Kigali risobanura amatsembabwoko menshi atandukanye
n'ibihe byayo. Ntiritanga ingero zose z'ibikorwa by'itsembabwoko kubera umwanya
muto udahagije. Iri murika rishobora gusa kwerekana ingero nkeya,
rigaragaza igice kibabaje cy'itsembatsemba n'itsembabwoko ry'ikinyejana.&
